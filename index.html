Our Menu

Chicken

Our chicken dishes are a celebration of flavor and texture, crafted with the finest, locally-sourced ingredients. Start with our signature grilled chicken breast, marinated in a blend of herbs and spices, and served with a side of garlic mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables. For those who crave something spicy, try our Cajun-style chicken, coated in a fiery blend of paprika, cayenne, and other secret spices, and served with a cooling cilantro-lime slaw. And for a creamy indulgence, our chicken Alfredo combines tender chicken with a rich, velvety Alfredo sauce, all atop a bed of fettuccine pasta. Each dish is a testament to our commitment to quality and taste.

Beef

Our beef selections are a carnivore's dream, featuring premium cuts of meat that are cooked to perfection. Begin with our slow-cooked beef stew, where chunks of tender beef are simmered in a rich, savory broth with root vegetables, creating a comfort food classic. For a more indulgent experience, our ribeye steak is a must-try. Grilled to your desired temperature and topped with a garlic-herb butter, it's served with a side of crispy fries and a fresh arugula salad. And for those who appreciate bold flavors, our Korean-style beef bulgogi is marinated in a sweet and savory sauce, then grilled and served with steamed rice and kimchi. Each bite is a journey of taste and texture.

Sushi

Our sushi menu is a culinary adventure, showcasing the freshest fish and most innovative combinations. Start with the classic California roll, featuring succulent crab meat, avocado, and cucumber, all wrapped in delicate rice and seaweed. For a taste of the exotic, try our Dragon roll, a harmonious blend of eel, avocado, and cucumber, topped with a sweet eel sauce and sesame seeds. Our sashimi selection offers a minimalist approach, with slices of pristine tuna, salmon, and yellowtail served with a side of pickled ginger and wasabi. Each piece of sushi is crafted with precision and care, ensuring a delightful and memorable dining experience.
